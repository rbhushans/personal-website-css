Mask Bot

I developed an automated Twitter bot which uses replies to tweets with images of groups of people that are not wearing masks. This was created during the COVID-19 pandemic, with the intention of trying to urge as many people to wear masks. The bot used a computer vision face mask detection model to identify faces without masks. The source code for the bot can be found here.
